Welcome to Numuyel Jaganara

We are a family of a lot of people. Each year, every individual contributes €100 if they reside in European countries, and $100 if they are in the United States. These funds are allocated to cover household expenses in our village, such as electricity, water, and other maintenance needs.

Spend

Here is a summary of the expenses covered by the contributions.

  • Electricity: €500
  • Water: €300
  • Maintenance: €200

History

Our family has been contributing for over 10 years to support our village.

We are proud of our shared effort and commitment.
